         Device information: This includes the timestamp of your app usage, system language set on your device, device model (e.g., iPhone 15 or Samsung Galaxy S24), screen resolution and display settings, device time zone, and operating system version (e.g., iOS 18 or Android 15). These logs are critical for monitoring the app’s operational status, fixing technical bugs (such as sudden app crashes), and continuously enhancing the user experience.

         Installed apps (with your explicit approval): To display video ads, we integrate the AppLovin SDK. At the app’s first launch, this SDK may request access to the list of apps installed on your device. If you do not wish to share this information, you can decline the request directly. Importantly, this information is only accessed by AppLovin; our team (the developer of Calm Ball Sort - Puzzle Therapy) has no access to or control over this data.

We implement a range of technical and administrative security measures to safeguard your Personal Information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure:

We retain your Personal Information only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with applicable legal requirements. Once the information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

You have the right to accept or refuse these cookies: most devices and browsers allow you to adjust cookie settings (e.g., block all cookies or receive a notification when a cookie is sent). Additionally, with your explicit permission to access your device’s contact list, we may upload your contact information to our servers for two specific purposes: (1) detecting fraudulent activities (e.g., preventing users from creating multiple accounts using the same contact details); and (2) synchronizing contact-related features (if the app includes such functions in the future, e.g., inviting contacts to play the app).
